Bulbasaur

Generation I - Pokemon

Bulbasaur resembles a small amphibian, but it bears three claws on each of its feet and has no tail. It also has large, red eyes and small, sharp teeth.

Each Bulbasaur has a "bulb" on its back that grows steadily larger as it matures. This bulb contains a seed which uses photosynthesis to supply Bulbasaur with energy.
